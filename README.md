# ShadowMed

A simple tool for tracking clinical shadowing hours, reflections, and experiences to help students stay organized as they explore medicine. It turns scattered time in clinics and hospitals into a clear record of progress toward a healthcare path.

## Built By Students

This was built by students who are also trying to navigate the path into medicine, dealing with the same problem of scattered notes, forgotten hours, and unorganized experiences across clinics and specialties. It exists to bring those moments into one place so they are easier to track, reflect on, and actually use later.

## What It Does

- Tracks shadowing hours by clinic, physician, and specialty  
- Logs short reflections from each experience  
- Keeps everything in one simple dashboard  
- Helps organize clinical exposure over time  

## Why It Exists

Shadowing experiences are easy to forget but important in shaping a future in healthcare. This keeps them organized so the focus stays on learning instead of keeping track of everything manually.

## Who Can Use It

Free for students. No paywalls or premium tiers.
